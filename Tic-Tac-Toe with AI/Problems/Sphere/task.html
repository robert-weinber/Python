Sphere Open on Hyperskill

In her projects, Jess works with geometrical objects. One of those objects is a sphere. There are 3 characteristics she needs in her projects: the pi number ( \(\approx 3.14\)), the radius r and the volume v of the particular sphere.

The volume is calculated according to this formula: \(v = \frac {4}{3} \pi r^3\). 

Create a class Sphere and then calculate the volume of the particular sphere.

The input format:

The value of r for a particular sphere.

The output format:

The volume v of this sphere. Round the number to 2 decimals.

[HINT]Constants are often stored in class variables.[/HINT]


Sample Input:
1
Sample Output:
4.19


Memory limit: 256 Mb
Time limit: 5s
