Who is who Open on Hyperskill

There are two classes: Angels and Demons. They have certain characteristics that help tell them apart.

You need to identify an individual by their property from the input. If it doesn't fit any profile, write No match.

Remember that characteristics are case sensitive!

The input format:

One line with the type of a characteristic and the value of that characteristic of an unknown individual separated by a whitespace.

The output format:

One line with the class of an individual: Angel, Demon or No match

[HINT]You can check the value of a particular attribute with the built-in getattr() function. In our example, it would look like this: getattr(Angel, "home")  # "Heaven". [/HINT]


Sample Input:
home Heaven
Sample Output:
Angel


Memory limit: 256 Mb
Time limit: 5s
